Since 2014 Phelia Barouh is the Co-Founder and Curator of the photography festival Foto Fabrika in Sofia, Bulgaria. She is currently working on a personal project and her first book called Palermo. Box. Me. as a result of an art residency in Palermo, Sicily. She has been studying in Brussels, Madrid, Sofia and Mexico City. Phelia Barouh is twice finalist of the Canon Picture of the Year contest in Sofia, Bulgaria and once of the EFTI photo contest in Madrid, Spain.
